Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Property Management VA services

What property management tasks can your VA handle?
▼
Our VAs handle tenant communications, maintenance coordination, listing management, move-in/out processing, accounting support, and general administrative tasks. They work within your systems and follow your procedures.
Do your VAs have property management experience?
▼
Yes. We vet candidates specifically for property management expertise. Our VAs understand industry workflows, common challenges, and best practices. They're trained on your specific systems during onboarding.
How quickly can my VA start?
▼
We typically have your VA ready to start within 2-3 days of your discovery call. This includes vetting, interviews, and initial onboarding setup.
What if my VA isn't a good fit?
▼
We replace and re-onboard at no cost. Your satisfaction is our priority. If the match isn't right, we'll find someone who is better suited to your needs.
Do you offer bilingual support?
▼
Yes. Many of our VAs are bilingual (English/Spanish). This is especially valuable for property managers serving diverse tenant populations.
What's the pricing structure?
▼
We offer flat-rate pricing starting at $1,300/month for 40 hours/week. No setup fees, no long-term contracts. Month-to-month flexibility with free replacements if not a good fit.
