# Insurance Customer Service Hero Image Requirements

## Overview
This document describes the image requirements for the Insurance Customer Service page hero section.

## Image Details

**File Name:** `insurance-customer-service-hero.webp`  
**Location:** `/public/images/Services/insurance-customer-service-hero.webp`  
**Format:** WebP (with fallback to JPG if needed)  
**Recommended Dimensions:** 1200x800px (minimum 800x600px)  
**Aspect Ratio:** 3:2 or 16:10

## Image Style & Content

### Style Reference
- Similar style to `customer-service-hero.webp` (general customer service image)
- Professional, modern, and approachable aesthetic
- Ocean brand colors (turquoise/teal tones) can be incorporated subtly

### Content Requirements

**Subject:** Insurance Customer Service Representative
- A different type of agent/representative than the one in `customer-service-hero.webp`
- Professional appearance
- Should convey expertise, trust, and care

**Setting:**
- Modern office or professional workspace
- Insurance-related context (can include subtle insurance elements like documents, computer with insurance software, etc.)
- Clean, organized environment

**Composition:**
- Agent should be the focal point
- Professional attire
- Engaging with client (can be on phone, video call, or reviewing documents)
- Warm, approachable expression
- Diverse representation preferred

**Mood:**
- Professional yet approachable
- Trustworthy and knowledgeable
- Caring and attentive
- Modern and efficient

## Technical Specifications

- **Resolution:** 1200x800px (or higher for retina displays)
- **Format:** WebP (optimized)
- **File Size:** < 200KB (optimized)
- **Color Space:** sRGB
- **Quality:** High quality, professional photography or illustration

## Usage

This image is used in:
- `src/InsuranceCostumerService.jsx` - Hero section

## Notes

- This image should be distinct from `customer-service-hero.webp` while maintaining visual consistency
- The agent should look professional and insurance-industry appropriate
- Consider showing the agent in a context that suggests insurance expertise (e.g., reviewing policy documents, using insurance software, etc.)
