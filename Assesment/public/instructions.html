System Overview

What this system does and why it works

üåä What This System Does

This quiz is a self-qualifying lead funnel that automatically:

  • Qualifies operational readiness (Can they successfully work with a VA?)
  • Measures buying intent (How close are they to purchasing?)
  • Detects urgency signals (Do they need help NOW?)
  • Segments automatically into 4 profiles (A, B, C, D)
  • Personalizes follow-up content (Different message for each profile)
  • Optimizes sales time (Only hot leads get calls)
