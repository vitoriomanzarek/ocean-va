# Ocean VA Agency Context

## Who We Are
Ocean VA is a premium Virtual Assistant agency connecting high-quality talent aligned with US time zones to businesses needing support. 
We focus on specialized, college-educated VAs who are not just task-doers but strategic partners.

## Our Services
- **Administrative Support**: Email management, scheduling, data entry.
- **Social Media Management**: Content creation, posting, engagement.
- **Sales & Lead Gen**: Cold calling, CRM management, outreach.
- **Specialized Roles**: Bookkeeping, Video Editing, Graphic Design.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Time Zone Aligned**: Efficiency without the lag.
- **Vetted Talent**: Top 1% of applicants.
- **Plug & Play**: We handle payroll, HR, and training support.

## Brand Voice
- Professional but approachable.
- Empowering and efficient.
- "We handle the chaos so you can handle the growth."
- Target Audience: Small business owners, busy executives, entrepreneurs.

## Key Terminology
- **"Ocean VA"**: Our brand name.
- **"VA"**: Virtual Assistant (use widely).
- **"Remote Talent"**: Synonym for VA.
- **"Growth Partner"**: How we position our VAs.
